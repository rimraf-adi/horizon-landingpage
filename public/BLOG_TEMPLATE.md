## Metadata Explanation

- **title**: Your blog post title (shows in preview cards and page)
- **date**: Publication date in YYYY-MM-DD format
- **description**: 150-200 character summary for preview cards and SEO
- **author**: Your name
- **tags**: Topics/keywords for categorization (optional)
- **readTime**: Estimated reading time (optional, e.g., "5 min read")
